Industry Ready Engineer

IndustrCons IRE-3 exists because engineering education and engineering work are taught in two different languages. Coursework teaches formulas and theory. A first job teaches judgment — reading a drawing under a deadline, defending a number to a manager, knowing which checklist item actually matters. IRE-3 is built to close that gap before the first job starts, not during it.

Every internship is a simulated role inside a fictional company, built around the real documents and decisions that role makes on an actual project: drawings, BOQs, method statements, RFIs, daily reports. Nothing here claims a partnership with a real construction firm — the companies are fictional, though their practices are informed by how leading international contractors operate.

IndustrCons was founded by Elvin Asgarov.
