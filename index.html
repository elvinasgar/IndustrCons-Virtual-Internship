Questions

Before you start

Is this affiliated with a real construction company?

No. Every company and project in IRE-3 is fictional. Internships reference international engineering practices for realism, but IndustrCons has no partnership with any named firm.

Do I need construction experience to start?

No — internships are labeled Beginner, Intermediate, or Advanced. Beginner tracks assume only foundational engineering coursework.

Is my progress saved if I close the browser?

Yes. Progress, XP, and certificates are stored locally in your browser. Clearing site data will reset progress, since there's no account server behind IRE-3.

Is the certificate a real qualification?

It's a verifiable record of the specific tasks and case studies you completed — a demonstration artifact, not an accredited professional qualification.
