My progress disappeared

Progress lives in your browser's local storage. It's cleared if you clear site data/cookies for this site, use a different browser or device, or open the site in a private/incognito window. Nothing is stored on a server, by design, so there's no way to recover it from elsewhere yet.

The certificate PDF download isn't working

PDF export loads two small libraries from a CDN at the moment you click Download. If you're offline, or a network policy blocks cdnjs.cloudflare.com, the export will fail — use the Print button instead, which works fully offline via your browser's print-to-PDF option.

Can I redo a task?

Not yet in this version — once submitted, a task's feedback is locked in so your record stays honest. A "redo" option is a natural next feature to add to the storage engine.

Why does the site only have one full internship?

All 15 roles are in the catalog with real metadata (skills, duration, difficulty), but only the Site Engineer internship has its full step-by-step content authored right now. Every internship is a plain JSON file under data/internships/ — adding the next one is a content task, not a rebuild.
