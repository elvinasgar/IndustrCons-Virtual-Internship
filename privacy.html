Privacy Policy

Draft — replace with counsel-reviewed policy before public launch.

IndustrCons IRE-3 stores your name, XP, badges, and internship progress in your browser's local storage. This information is not transmitted to any IndustrCons server, because the current version of the platform has no backend.

Downloading a certificate as a PDF uses two third-party libraries (html2canvas and jsPDF) loaded from a public CDN (cdnjs.cloudflare.com) at the moment you click Download — no certificate data is sent to that CDN, only the library code is fetched from it.

Clearing your browser's site data will permanently delete your locally stored progress.
